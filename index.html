About Me

Hi, my name is Sarita, and I am currently in my 4th year at the University of Colorado, Boulder. My academic focus is a Bachelor of Arts in Vocal Performance along with a minor in Creative Technology & Design, a minor in Business, a Certificate in Music Technology, and a Certificate in Music Entrepreneurship. I spent the Spring semester of 2022 studying abroad at the National University of Singapore (NUS).

I began singing at a very young age and have additionally picked up piano and guitar over the years. I am learning the foundations of music production (Logic Pro and other recording techniques) while also exploring writing and producing my own music. In addition to my love for music, I am passionate about digital design and media editing (photo/video) and have both work and freelance experience in the field.

Apart from my academic and career goals I enjoy spending quality time with my friends and family, reading, listening to music, and spending time outdoors here in Boulder.
